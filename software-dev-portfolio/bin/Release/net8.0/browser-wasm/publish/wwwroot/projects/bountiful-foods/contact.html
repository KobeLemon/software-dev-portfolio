About Us!

We are centered in Carlsbad, California, but we have many food trucks up and down the California Coast so just look around and we are sure you will find us not too far away!

Carlsbad is a coastal city in the North County region of San Diego County, California. Carlsbad is 87 miles south of downtown Los Angeles and 35 miles north of downtown San Diego. As of 2020, our population is 114,746. and we are dedicated to serving every single one! Carlsbad is a popular tourist destination and home to many businesses

Bountiful Foods was founded on the ideal of good food and healthy living. Our goal is to bring healthy, organic food to the people throughout San Diego County and to provide a happy and welcoming place for everyone.

Thank you so much for visiting us and we promise that every single ingredient we use is organic and locally and ethically sourced. Come visit us and we will give you a meal you will never forget!
